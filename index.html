What is the Carbon Cycle?
The carbon cycle is a cycle of carbon (in the form of CO2) being added or taken from the atmosphere balancing it out The processes are usually done through processes, such as: combustion, AI generation etc.. The cycle is the balance of CO2 getting removed and added. The problem is the imbalance of carbon in the atmosphere. Too much or a lack of CO2 will kill off most of the aerobic life on earth. The problem right now is that we are producing too much CO2. This is through ways such as combustion, burning and exhaling. On the contrary the way that CO2 is used is mainly in photosynthesis for plants but due to deforestation that isn't removing enough carbon from the atmosphere (kmansfield).
